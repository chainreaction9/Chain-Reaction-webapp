It is a turn-based multi-player board game where the goal is to eliminate other players from the board.

Basic Rules

  • The players have a unique color and take turn in placing orbs of their color at either an empty cell or a cell already occupied by them.
  • Each cell has a maximum capacity : A cell can contain at most one less than the number of its neighbouring cells. The cells that are not on an edge or a corner of the board can contain most number of orbs.
  • A fully occupied cell is an explosive: adding one more orb of the same color will cause it to explode and each neighbouring cell will receive one more orb of the same color after the explosion.
    So if you cause an explosion, all the neighbouring cells will become yours, even if they were already occupied by other players!
    If a neighbouring cell contained other player's explosive, your explosion will cause them to explode too and create a chain reaction!
  • You win by eliminating all other players from the board.
